## Motivation

LLM驱动的个人助手在记忆用户消息并响应个性化查询方面展现出巨大潜力。然而，目前缺乏对它们记忆能力进行客观、自动评估的方法。

主要挑战在于难以根据用户消息构建可靠的问答（QAs）对，尤其是在保证多样性和可扩展性的同时。现有的评估方法往往依赖人工标注，效率低下且难以大规模应用。

## Contribution

- **贝叶斯模拟器MemSim**: 提出了一个新颖的贝叶斯模拟器，能够自动从生成的LLM用户消息中构建可靠的问答对，同时确保其多样性和可扩展性。
- **贝叶斯关系网络（BRNet）**: 引入了BRNet和因果生成机制，以减轻LLM幻觉对事实信息的影响，从而促进评估数据集的自动创建。
- **MemDaily数据集**: 基于MemSim生成了一个日常场景下的数据集MemDaily，并进行了广泛实验以评估方法的有效性。
- **记忆机制基准**: 提供了使用MemDaily数据集评估LLM代理中不同记忆机制的基准。
- **项目开源**: 为研究社区开源了项目代码和数据集，促进后续研究。

## Method

MemSim的核心在于其自动生成可靠、多样且可扩展的问答对的能力。

### 贝叶斯关系网络 (BRNet)
1.  **构建BRNet**: BRNet用于建模用户消息中的实体和它们之间的关系，以结构化的方式表示信息。
2.  **缓解幻觉**: 通过BRNet的结构化表示和因果生成机制，减少LLM在生成问答对时产生幻觉的可能性，确保事实准确性。

### 因果生成机制
1.  **消息生成**: LLM首先根据预设场景生成用户消息序列，模拟真实的日常对话。
2.  **问答对构建**: 基于生成的BRNet和消息，系统自动生成多样的问答对，涵盖单跳、多跳、比较、聚合和后处理等多种复杂类型。
3.  **可靠性验证**: 生成的问答对通过与BRNet中的事实信息进行比对来验证其可靠性，确保问题有准确的答案。

- **vs. 现有LLM评估基准**: 现有基准在构建可靠和多样化的问答对方面存在挑战，MemSim通过引入BRNet和因果生成机制，有效缓解了LLM幻觉问题，提升了数据集的质量。

## Motivation

LLM-based personal assistants show great promise in memorizing user information and responding to personalized queries. However, there is a current lack of objective and automatic evaluation methods for their memory capabilities.

A major challenge lies in constructing reliable question-answer (QA) pairs from user messages, especially while ensuring diversity and scalability. Existing evaluation approaches often rely on manual annotation, which is inefficient and difficult to scale.

## Contribution

- **Bayesian Simulator MemSim**: Proposed a novel Bayesian simulator designed to automatically construct reliable QA pairs from generated user messages, simultaneously maintaining their diversity and scalability.
- **Bayesian Relation Network (BRNet)**: Introduced BRNet and a causal generation mechanism to mitigate the impact of LLM hallucinations on factual information, facilitating the automatic creation of an evaluation dataset.
- **MemDaily Dataset**: Generated a daily-life scenario dataset, MemDaily, based on MemSim, and conducted extensive experiments to assess the effectiveness of our approach.
- **Memory Mechanism Benchmark**: Provided a benchmark for evaluating different memory mechanisms in LLM-based agents using the MemDaily dataset.
- **Open-source Project**: Released the project code and dataset to the research community to foster further research.

## Method

MemSim's core strength lies in its ability to automatically generate reliable, diverse, and scalable QA pairs.

### Bayesian Relation Network (BRNet)
1.  **Constructing BRNet**: BRNet is used to model entities and their relationships within user messages, representing information in a structured manner.
2.  **Mitigating Hallucinations**: Through BRNet's structured representation and a causal generation mechanism, the likelihood of LLM hallucinations during QA generation is reduced, ensuring factual accuracy.

### Causal Generation Mechanism
1.  **Message Generation**: An LLM first generates sequences of user messages based on predefined scenarios, simulating real-world daily conversations.
2.  **QA Pair Construction**: Based on the generated BRNet and messages, the system automatically constructs diverse QA pairs, covering various complex types such as single-hop, multi-hop, comparative, aggregative, and post-processing.
3.  **Reliability Verification**: The generated QA pairs are validated for reliability by comparing them against factual information within the BRNet, ensuring that questions have accurate answers.

## Comparison with Previous Work

- **vs. Traditional Manual Annotation**: MemSim automates QA pair generation, significantly improving evaluation efficiency and scalability, and avoiding the time-consuming and subjective nature of manual annotation.
- **vs. Existing LLM Evaluation Benchmarks**: Existing benchmarks face challenges in constructing reliable and diverse QA pairs. MemSim, by introducing BRNet and a causal generation mechanism, effectively mitigates LLM hallucination issues, enhancing dataset quality.
- **vs. LLM-only QA Generation**: Solely relying on LLMs for QA generation is prone to hallucinations. MemSim provides factual anchoring through its Bayesian Relation Network, ensuring the reliability of the QA pairs.
