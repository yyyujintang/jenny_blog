## Motivation

现有AI代理的长期记忆基准主要依赖多轮对话或合成用户历史，这使得检索性能不能完全反映对人物的理解能力。

这些基准往往侧重于事实性召回，而忽略了对人物动机、决策原则和主观状态的深层推理。

为了构建真正能理解人类的数字伙伴，我们需要一个能评估AI在复杂叙事中进行高层次推理的基准。

## Contribution

- **新颖的基准数据集**: KnowMe-Bench基于长篇自传叙事构建，包含丰富的行动、上下文和内心想法，为推断稳定动机和决策原则提供了密集证据。
- **多层次评估体系**: 评估问题涵盖事实召回 (factual recall)、主观状态归因 (subjective state attribution) 和原则级推理 (principle-level reasoning)，全面衡量AI对人物的理解。
- **时间锚定与闪回感知**: 数据集将叙事重建为闪回感知 (flashback-aware)、时间锚定 (time-anchored) 的事件流，更好地处理复杂的时间结构。
- **公开可用的资源**: KnowMe-Bench数据集及其代码已公开，促进了社区在长期人物理解领域的研究。

## Method

### 数据集构建
1.  **叙事选择**: 从多样化的长篇自传叙事中选取原始文本。
2.  **事件流重建**: 将叙事内容解析并重建为闪回感知、时间锚定的事件流，确保事件的逻辑和时间顺序。
3.  **证据链接**: 为每个事件流中的元素（行动、上下文、内心想法）建立与原始叙事的链接，作为推理的证据。

### 问题生成与标注
1.  **问题类型**: 设计了三类问题：
    *   **事实召回 (Factual Recall)**: 考察对叙事中具体事实的记忆。
    *   **主观状态归因 (Subjective State Attribution)**: 评估理解人物情感、意图和信念的能力。
    *   **原则级推理 (Principle-level Reasoning)**: 挑战AI从叙事中推断人物稳定动机和决策原则的能力。
2.  **证据链接**: 每个问题都与叙事中的特定证据片段关联，用于评估模型回答的准确性和支持度。

### 评估指标
1.  **准确性**: 衡量模型回答的正确性。
2.  **证据支持度**: 评估模型回答是否能被提供的证据有效支持。

## Motivation

Existing long-horizon memory benchmarks for AI agents primarily rely on multi-turn dialogues or synthetic user histories. This approach makes retrieval performance an imperfect proxy for genuine person understanding.

These benchmarks often prioritize factual recall, neglecting deeper inference capabilities related to motivations, decision principles, and subjective states of individuals.

To build digital companions that truly understand humans, there's a critical need for a benchmark capable of evaluating AI's higher-level reasoning within complex narratives.

## Contribution

- **Novel Benchmark Dataset**: KnowMe-Bench is constructed from long-form autobiographical narratives, providing rich evidence of actions, context, and inner thoughts for inferring stable motivations and decision principles.
- **Multi-level Evaluation Framework**: The benchmark includes questions spanning factual recall, subjective state attribution, and principle-level reasoning, offering a comprehensive assessment of AI's person understanding.
- **Flashback-Aware & Time-Anchored Stream**: The dataset reconstructs each narrative into a flashback-aware, time-anchored event stream, better handling complex temporal structures.
- **Publicly Releasable Resource**: The KnowMe-Bench dataset and its code are publicly available, fostering research in the field of long-term person understanding.

## Method

### Dataset Construction
1.  **Narrative Selection**: Raw texts are chosen from diverse long-form autobiographical narratives.
2.  **Event Stream Reconstruction**: Narratives are parsed and reconstructed into a flashback-aware, time-anchored stream of events, ensuring logical and chronological order.
3.  **Evidence Linking**: Each element within the event stream (actions, context, inner thoughts) is linked back to specific segments in the original narrative, serving as evidence for reasoning.

### Question Generation and Annotation
1.  **Question Types**: Three categories of questions are designed:
    *   **Factual Recall**: Tests memory of specific facts within the narrative.
    *   **Subjective State Attribution**: Evaluates the ability to understand a person's emotions, intentions, and beliefs.
    *   **Principle-level Reasoning**: Challenges the AI to infer stable motivations and decision principles from the narrative.
2.  **Evidence Linking**: Each question is linked to specific evidence snippets within the narrative, used to evaluate the accuracy and support of model answers.

### Evaluation Metrics
1.  **Accuracy**: Measures the correctness of the model's answers.
2.  **Evidence Support**: Assesses whether the model's answers are effectively supported by the provided evidence.

## Comparison with Previous Work

- **vs. LongMemEval (Wu et al., 2025)**: While existing benchmarks often use multi-turn dialogues or synthetic user histories, KnowMe-Bench focuses on long-form autobiographical narratives, offering richer and more authentic context.
- **vs. LoCoMo (Maharana et al., 2024b)**: Although LoCoMo also addresses long conversations, KnowMe-Bench delves into the inner world and stable principles of individuals through autobiographical narratives, going beyond mere conversational consistency.
- **vs. MemBench (Tan et al., 2025)**: MemBench focuses on factual and reflective memory behaviors in interactive agent scenarios, whereas KnowMe-Bench places a stronger emphasis on higher-level reasoning about motivations and decision principles.
- **vs. CloneMem (Hu et al., 2026)**: CloneMem uses personal traces like diaries and social posts to track evolving personal states. KnowMe-Bench, through structured autobiographical narratives, provides a more systematic evaluation of deep person understanding.
