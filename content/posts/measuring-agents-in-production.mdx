## Motivation

目前，LLM代理已在多个行业投入生产，但我们对其成功部署的技术方法知之甚少。
现有研究缺乏对生产环境中代理的真实理解，多集中于高层行业报告、学术综述或特定应用演示，缺乏第一手数据。
这种知识空白阻碍了研究社区了解实际部署的挑战和未被充分探索的研究方向。

## Motivation

LLM-based agents are already operating in production across many industries, yet we lack a clear understanding of the technical methods that contribute to their successful deployment.
Existing research often consists of high-level industry accounts, academic surveys of existing literature, or application-specific demonstrations, without providing first-hand data from production environments.
This knowledge gap prevents the research community from gaining visibility into deployment realities and identifying under-explored research avenues.

## Contribution

- **First Systematic Study (MAP)**: Presents the first systematic study of LLM agents in production, named MAP (Measuring Agents in Production).
- **First-Hand Data Collection**: Gathers first-hand data from agent developers through 20 in-depth interview case studies and a survey of 306 practitioners across 26 domains.
- **Revealing Agent Construction Practices**: Finds that production agents are built using simple, controllable approaches: 68% execute at most 10 steps before human intervention, and 70% rely on prompting off-the-shelf models instead of weight tuning.
- **Clarifying Evaluation Methods**: Discovers that 74% of production agents primarily depend on human evaluation.
- **Identifying Core Challenges**: Identifies reliability (consistent correct behavior over time) as the top development challenge, which practitioners currently address through systems-level design.
- **Providing Research Directions**: Offers the research community visibility into deployment realities and highlights under-explored research avenues.

## Method

### Data Collection
1.  **Case Studies**:
    *   Conducted 20 in-depth interview case studies to gather detailed insights into the agent development process.
    *   Interviews covered why organizations build agents, how they build them, how they evaluate them, and their top development challenges.
2.  **Large-Scale Survey**:
    *   Surveyed 306 practitioners from 26 different domains.
    *   The survey aimed to collect broader quantitative data to complement the qualitative findings from the case studies.

### Data Analysis
1.  **Construction Method Analysis**:
    *   Analyzed the number of steps agents execute before human intervention and their reliance on prompting versus weight tuning.
    *   Quantified the trend towards simple, controllable approaches in production agents.
2.  **Evaluation Strategy Analysis**:
    *   Investigated the proportion of human versus automated evaluation used for agents.
    *   Determined the predominant role of human evaluation in current production agents.
3.  **Challenge Identification**:
    *   Identified and categorized the main challenges faced in agent development through both interviews and surveys.
    *   Focused particularly on reliability issues and current solutions (systems-level design).
