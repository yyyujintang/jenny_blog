## Motivation

尽管近期在长序列视频生成方面取得了进展，但现有框架仍面临显著局限。这些局限主要体现在辅助能力差、视觉质量不佳以及表达力有限。

用户通常难以将抽象的想法高效转化为具体的视觉叙事。现有的生成模型在处理复杂、连贯的长序列视频时，往往难以保持一致性和高质量。

MAViS旨在通过多智能体协作框架来缓解这些问题，从而更有效地将想法转化为视觉叙事。

## Contribution

- **多智能体协作框架 (MAViS)**：提出一个多智能体协作框架，专门用于辅助长序列视频叙事生成。
- **3E 原则**：引入 Explore (探索)、Examine (检查) 和 Enhance (增强) 原则，确保各阶段中间输出的完整性。
- **剧本编写指南 (Script Writing Guidelines)**：为优化剧本与生成工具的兼容性，提出了专门的剧本编写指南。
- **模块化框架**：MAViS 的模块化设计使其能够灵活扩展，兼容多种生成模型和工具。
- **多模态设计输出**：作为目前唯一能提供包含叙事和背景音乐的视频框架，实现了多模态输出。

## Method

MAViS 框架通过以下多阶段协作流程实现长序列视频叙事生成：

1.  **剧本编写 (Script Writing)**：
    *   智能体根据用户提供的简要想法描述，生成详细的视频剧本。
    *   遵循 Script Writing Guidelines，确保剧本与后续生成工具的兼容性。
2.  **镜头设计 (Shot Designing)**：
    *   智能体根据剧本内容，设计每个镜头的构图、角度和场景布局。
    *   在这一阶段，智能体运用 3E 原则来探索多种设计方案，检查其完整性，并进行增强。
3.  **角色建模 (Character Modeling)**：
    *   智能体根据剧本和镜头设计，创建或选择视频中的角色模型。
4.  **关键帧生成 (Keyframe Generation)**：
    *   智能体生成视频的关键帧图像，作为后续动画的基础。
5.  **视频动画 (Video Animation)**：
    *   智能体将关键帧转化为流畅的视频动画序列。
6.  **音频生成 (Audio Generation)**：
    *   智能体为生成的视频添加叙事旁白和背景音乐，实现多模态输出。

## Motivation

Despite recent advances, existing long-sequence video generation frameworks still suffer from significant limitations. These primarily include poor assistive capability, suboptimal visual quality, and limited expressiveness.

Users often struggle to efficiently translate abstract ideas into concrete visual narratives. Current generative models frequently fail to maintain consistency and high quality when handling complex, coherent long-sequence videos.

MAViS aims to mitigate these issues through a multi-agent collaborative framework, thereby more effectively translating ideas into visual narratives.

## Contribution

-   **Multi-Agent Collaborative Framework (MAViS)**: Proposes a multi-agent collaborative framework specifically designed to assist in long-sequence video storytelling.
-   **3E Principle**: Introduces the Explore, Examine, and Enhance principle to ensure the completeness of intermediate outputs at each stage.
-   **Script Writing Guidelines**: Develops specific guidelines for script writing to optimize compatibility between scripts and generative tools.
-   **Modular Framework**: MAViS's modular design allows for flexible scalability with diverse generative models and tools.
-   **Multimodal Design Output**: Achieves multimodal output as the only framework currently providing videos with narratives and background music.

## Method

The MAViS framework achieves long-sequence video storytelling through the following multi-stage collaborative process:

1.  **Script Writing**:
    *   Agents generate a detailed video script based on a brief idea description provided by the user.
    *   The Script Writing Guidelines are followed to ensure compatibility between the script and subsequent generative tools.
2.  **Shot Designing**:
    *   Agents design the composition, angles, and scene layouts for each shot based on the script.
    *   During this stage, agents apply the 3E Principle to explore various design options, examine their completeness, and enhance them.
3.  **Character Modeling**:
    *   Agents create or select character models for the video based on the script and shot designs.
4.  **Keyframe Generation**:
    *   Agents generate keyframe images for the video, serving as the foundation for subsequent animation.
5.  **Video Animation**:
    *   Agents transform the keyframes into a fluid sequence of video animation.
6.  **Audio Generation**:
    *   Agents add narrative voice-overs and background music to the generated video, achieving multimodal output.
