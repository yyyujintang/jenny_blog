## Motivation

现有研究主要关注不超过五个对话会话的长期开放域对话，这限制了我们对LLM在更长时间跨度内记忆能力的理解。
尽管长上下文LLM和RAG技术有所进步，但它们在超长期对话中的有效性尚未得到充分探索。
当前缺乏一个专门用于评估LLM在多会话、多模态、超长期对话中记忆能力的高质量数据集和评估基准。

## Contribution

- **LoCoMo数据集**: 一个高质量的超长期对话数据集，每段对话平均包含300轮次和9K tokens，跨越多达35个会话，并支持多模态（图像共享）。
- **机器-人工生成流程**: 提出一种利用LLM代理架构，并基于角色和时间事件图生成对话的流水线，通过人工标注进行验证和编辑，确保长程一致性和事件图的接地性。
- **综合评估基准**: 基于LoCoMo数据集，建立了一个全面的评估基准，用于衡量模型在问答、事件摘要和多模态对话生成任务中的长期记忆能力。
- **深入实验分析**: 揭示了LLM在理解长对话以及对话中长程时间与因果动态方面的挑战，并指出长上下文LLM和RAG虽有改进，但仍远低于人类表现。

## Method

### 数据集生成流程
1.  **LLM代理架构**: 利用LLM构建对话代理，使其能够基于预设的角色（personas）和时间事件图进行对话。
2.  **多模态能力**: 为每个代理配备共享和响应图像的能力，增加对话的丰富性。
3.  **对话生成**: 代理根据角色和事件图进行自由对话，生成初步的超长期对话。
4.  **人工验证与编辑**: 招募人类标注员对生成的对话进行验证和编辑，确保对话的长程一致性、与事件图的准确对应以及整体流畅性。

### 评估基准任务
1.  **问答 (Question Answering)**: 评估模型从超长对话中检索和理解信息的能力，包括单跳、多跳、时间推理、开放域和对抗性问题。
2.  **事件摘要 (Event Summarization)**: 评估模型从对话中提取关键事件并进行准确摘要的能力，这需要对对话的因果和时间动态有深刻理解。
3.  **多模态对话生成 (Multi-modal Dialogue Generation)**: 评估模型在多模态上下文（包含图像）中生成连贯、相关对话响应的能力。

## Comparison with Previous Work

- **vs. 现有长期对话数据集**:
    - 现有数据集（如MPChat, MMDialog, Daily Dialog, SODA, MSC, Conversation Chronicles）的对话长度、会话数量和时间跨度远小于LoCoMo。
    - LoCoMo平均每段对话300轮次，35个会话，9K tokens，而多数现有数据集仅有几轮对话和单会话。
    - LoCoMo支持多模态交互，而许多现有数据集（如Daily Dialog, SODA, MSC, Conversation Chronicles）则不支持。
- **vs. 仅依赖LLM生成的数据集**:
    - LoCoMo采用机器-人工混合流水线，确保了数据的高质量和长程一致性，弥补了纯LLM生成数据可能存在的连贯性问题。
- **vs. 传统RAG和长上下文LLM评估**:
    - 本文的评估基准专门针对“超长期”对话记忆，超越了传统RAG和长上下文LLM通常评估的“五会话以内”的范围，揭示了它们在更长跨度下的局限性。

## Motivation

Existing research on long-term open-domain dialogues primarily evaluates model responses within contexts spanning no more than five chat sessions, limiting our understanding of LLM memory over longer periods.
Despite advancements in long-context LLMs and RAG techniques, their efficacy in very long-term dialogues remains largely unexplored.
There is a current lack of high-quality datasets and benchmarks specifically designed to evaluate LLMs' memory capabilities in multi-session, multi-modal, very long-term conversations.

## Contribution

- **LoCoMo Dataset**: A high-quality dataset for very long-term conversations, with each conversation averaging 300 turns and 9K tokens, spanning up to 35 sessions, and supporting multimodal interactions (image sharing).
- **Machine-Human Generation Pipeline**: Introduction of a pipeline that leverages LLM-based agent architectures, grounding dialogues on personas and temporal event graphs, with human annotators verifying and editing for long-range consistency and grounding.
- **Comprehensive Evaluation Benchmark**: Establishment of a robust benchmark based on LoCoMo, designed to measure long-term memory in models across question answering, event summarization, and multi-modal dialogue generation tasks.
- **In-depth Experimental Analysis**: Revelation of LLMs' challenges in understanding lengthy conversations and comprehending long-range temporal and causal dynamics within dialogues, indicating that while long-context LLMs and RAG offer improvements, they still substantially lag behind human performance.

## Method

### Dataset Generation Pipeline
1.  **LLM Agent Architecture**: LLM-based agents are designed to conduct dialogues grounded in predefined personas and temporal event graphs.
2.  **Multimodal Capability**: Each agent is equipped with the ability to share and react to images, enhancing the richness of conversations.
3.  **Dialogue Generation**: Agents engage in free-form conversations based on their personas and event graphs to generate initial very long-term dialogues.
4.  **Human Verification and Editing**: Human annotators verify and edit the generated conversations to ensure long-range consistency, accurate grounding to event graphs, and overall fluency.

### Evaluation Benchmark Tasks
1.  **Question Answering**: Assesses the model's ability to retrieve and comprehend information from very long dialogues, covering single-hop, multi-hop, temporal, open-domain, and adversarial questions.
2.  **Event Summarization**: Evaluates the model's capability to extract key events from conversations and summarize them accurately, requiring a deep understanding of causal and temporal dynamics.
3.  **Multi-modal Dialogue Generation**: Measures the model's performance in generating coherent and relevant dialogue responses within multimodal contexts, including images.

## Comparison with Previous Work

- **vs. Existing Long-Term Dialogue Datasets**:
    - Current datasets (e.g., MPChat, MMDialog, Daily Dialog, SODA, MSC, Conversation Chronicles) have significantly shorter dialogue lengths, fewer sessions, and smaller time spans compared to LoCoMo.
    - LoCoMo features an average of 300 turns, 35 sessions, and 9K tokens per conversation, whereas most existing datasets involve only a few turns and single sessions.
    - LoCoMo supports multimodal interactions, a feature often absent in many prior datasets (e.g., Daily Dialog, SODA, MSC, Conversation Chronicles).
- **vs. LLM-Generated-Only Datasets**:
    - LoCoMo employs a hybrid machine-human pipeline, ensuring high data quality and long-range consistency, addressing potential coherence issues found in purely LLM-generated data.
- **vs. Traditional RAG and Long-Context LLM Evaluations**:
    - This benchmark specifically targets "very long-term" conversational memory, extending beyond the "within five sessions" scope typically evaluated by traditional RAG and long-context LLMs, revealing their limitations over extended durations.
